Stephanie

Frontend Developer

Bio

I'm Stephanie, a frontend developer passionate about creating clean, responsive, and accessible web interfaces. I enjoy turning ideas into interactive experiences that feel intuitive and look well-balanced across all devices.

My journey started from curiosity, a simple fascination with how websites worked, and has grown into a love for building digital experiences that people can enjoy and easily navigate.

Goals in This Program

My goal is to grow as a frontend developer by learning to write cleaner, more scalable code and deepening my understanding of accessibility and design structure. I want to feel more confident turning creative ideas into real, functional projects that people can actually use and enjoy.

Areas of Low Confidence

I sometimes find debugging complex layout issues and ensuring full responsiveness across different screen sizes challenging. I'm also working on improving my confidence in structuring JavaScript logic efficiently, especially when building interactive components.
