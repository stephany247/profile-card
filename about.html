Stephanie

Frontend Developer

Bio

I’m Stephanie, a frontend developer passionate about creating clean, responsive, and accessible web interfaces. I enjoy turning ideas into interactive experiences that feel intuitive and look well-balanced across all devices.

My journey started from curiosity — a simple fascination with how websites worked — and has grown into a love for building digital experiences that people can enjoy and easily navigate.

Goals in This Program

My main goal is to sharpen my frontend development skills — learning to write cleaner, scalable code while mastering modern practices in accessibility, layout design, and component structure. I also want to build more confidence in transforming design ideas into production-ready projects.

Areas of Low Confidence

I sometimes struggle with debugging complex layout issues and ensuring full responsiveness across all screen sizes. I’m also working to improve my confidence in structuring JavaScript logic efficiently — especially when handling interactive components.

Note to Future Self
